Squirrels in Central Park



We are trying to keep track of the squirrels in the Central Park. You can find squirrels' positions in the map and their detailed information on the sightings page. You may also update their information by clicking on their ID link. We provide a statistic summary of current data in the Statistics page; and you are welcome to add new squirrels to the dataset in the Add a New Squirrel page.
