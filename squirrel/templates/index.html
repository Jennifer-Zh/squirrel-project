Squirrels in Central Park



We are trying to keep track of the squirrels found in the Central Park. Clicking the "Squirrels Map" button, you can find the squirrels' positions in the map. Clicking the "Sightings" button, you may see a list of all squirrels with their unique ID and the date the squirrel is recorded. You may also update their information (such as location, shift, primary fur color...) by clicking on their ID link. Clicking the "Statistics" button, you could find a statistical summary of recorded squirrels. At last, you are welcome to add new squirrels to the dataset by clicking the "Add a New Squirrel" button.

This open data is from City of New York. The name is "2018 Central Park Squirrel Census - Squirrel Data".
